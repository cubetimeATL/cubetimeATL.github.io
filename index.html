Who We Are

We are Cube Time

Cube Time is a powerhouse of two young speed cube solvers passionate about bringing cubing to the next generation. Our goal is to be in schools providing students with everything they need to become the next world record holder. Be it a good cube, community, or someone to push them to do better, we aim to be there every step of the way.

Why Speed Cubing

Learning to solve a Rubik's Cube is an incredibly effective way to teach patience at a young age. Because it's impossible to become a master overnight, it forces kids to perservere if they want to see results. It's also an amzing way for kids to learn about their minds at a young age. When you learn to solve a cube, in reality you're only supplied with the tools. It's the kids who have to figure out how to best get them to stick in their mind. They're given an opportunity to figure out how they learn best.
